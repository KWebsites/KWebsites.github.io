Social Responsibility

Empowerment
Empowerment is the process of increasing the capacity of individuals or groups to make choices and to transform those choices into desired actions and outcomes. Central to this process are actions which build both individual and collective assets and improve efficiency. We advocate these principles to our staff, suppliers and clients.
Giving Back

In developing a family-like culture, Plan-IT strives to support and give back to our communities.

  • Hottentots Animal Welfare Centre
  • RAD (Rescue Animal Drive)
  • Happy Valley Soup Kitchen
  • LIONS
  • Catholic Welfare Development
Growth
Through in-service training, Plan-IT are geared towards introducing new and innovative minds into the ICT and Development arena. We provide internships and on-the-job training to aspiring students.
