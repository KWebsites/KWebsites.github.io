Property Maintenence

S.P.S has a strong focus on providing property owners, managers, and body corporates with maintenance solutions for the caretaking of their properties. These solutions include Preventative-, Responsive- and Ad Hoc Maintenance as well as project planning and management.

For clients that want to have full confidence in knowing that their property portfolio is taken care of, we offer long term contracts. For a monthly fee, these contracts include the establishment of maintenance schedules and quick response times for unforeseen events. Any additional maintenance is quoted and approved before billed to the established budget.
